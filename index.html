Intelligent Systems for Next Generation Drone Functionality

Research Focus: Lab-to-Field Robotics using Human-Drone Interaction and Crossreality

This thesis explores the development of intelligent systems that enable seamless human-drone collaboration in both laboratory and field environments, investigating crossreality technologies to bridge the gap between controlled lab settings and real-world applications.
