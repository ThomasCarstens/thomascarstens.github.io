About

A bicultural French and South African. Current activity is as a software integration consultant. Currently based in Paris and exploring opportunities internationally.

In brief: engineering creative and techno-optimist regarding education & healthcare. Studied a Bachelors in Mechanical & Mechatronic Eng and did a Masters Degree in Creative Tech with a focus on Robotic Software. Since then, several years engineering experience as an R&D intern for sensor DAQs and freelance work in UAV mission planning. My passion for STEM education led me to student tutoring and developing an online course for Code4Kids. I have otherwise contributed to research on tertiary STEM education by deploying an innovative class forum. In my masters research I explored human-drone interaction: benchmarking novel usecases for human-drone collaboration in the lab and in the field.
